Prepworks 3-Piece Canning Essentials Kit

This all-in-one canning set is perfect for home cooks and food lovers who want to preserve their favorite recipes with confidence and ease. Whether you're preparing jams, sauces, or preserving vegetables, this kit simplifies the entire process and enhances your canning experience.

One-Handed Jar Lifter

Designed for safe and effortless jar handling, this lifter makes it easy to remove jars from boiling water or place them back into their storage. It keeps your hands away from hot surfaces and reduces the risk of burns during every step of the canning process.

Magnetic Lid Lifter

The magnetic lid lifter is a game-changer that lets you place lids on jars without touching them with your hands. This not only keeps your workspace clean but also ensures a safe and smooth lid application to your jars.

Canning Funnel with Headspace Measurement

This funnel is ideal for filling jars with precision, whether they are wide or regular mouth. The built-in headspace measurement helps ensure consistent fill levels, reducing air pockets and increasing the success rate of your preserves.

Durable & Dishwasher Safe

All tools are crafted from high-quality, long-lasting materials and are dishwasher safe for easy cleaning. This makes the kit a reliable and practical choice for both new and experienced canners, offering convenience without compromising quality.

With its efficient design and user-friendly tools, this 3-piece canning set is a great investment for anyone who loves home canning. It combines functionality, safety, and style to help you save time while keeping your kitchen organized and your preserves fresh.
